The Knomo Philosophy: Smart, Stylish, Functional

Knomo (short for Knowledge + Mobility) is a London-based brand founded on a simple belief: you shouldn’t have to sacrifice style for practicality. Their bags and backpacks are crafted to help people stay organized and mobile without compromising on aesthetics.

From sleek leather finishes to RFID-protected compartments, Knomo bags are engineered with the urban commuter in mind. They’re perfect for storing laptops, tech accessories, documents, chargers, and other essentials in a neatly organized way—without adding unnecessary bulk.

Why Choose Knomo Backpacks for Your Commute?

1. Smart Storage and Organization

A cluttered bag can make your day frustrating. Knomo backpacks are built with intelligent compartments that make it easy to find everything you need without digging. From padded laptop sleeves to hidden zipper pockets for valuables, every detail is thoughtfully considered.

Most Knomo backpacks include dedicated spaces for your laptop (up to 15”), tablet, smartphone, power bank, water bottle, and daily documents. Some models even come with detachable organizers, so you can grab your essentials and go without taking the entire bag.

2. Technology-Ready Design

Knomo backpacks are specifically designed to keep your tech protected and accessible. The shockproof laptop compartments and water-resistant exteriors ensure your devices stay safe, even during rainy commutes or crowded trains.

Many models also feature built-in RFID-blocking pockets, which prevent digital theft by shielding your credit cards and passport from unauthorized scanning—a valuable feature for travelers and professionals on the move.

3. Stylish Without Compromise

Gone are the days when “tech bags” looked overly utilitarian or uninspired. Knomo’s design philosophy blends classic craftsmanship with contemporary flair. Available in leather, canvas, and other durable materials, their backpacks come in a variety of sleek styles and colors suitable for both men and women.

Whether you prefer a minimalist look or something with a bit more character, there's a Knomo backpack to suit your aesthetic.

4. Built to Last

Investing in a Knomo backpack means choosing quality. These bags are crafted from premium materials such as full-grain leather, water-resistant nylon, and brushed metal hardware. Strong stitching and durable zippers ensure your bag will last through countless commutes, meetings, and miles of travel.
